Nutritional Guidelines

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Make sure your diet includes all essential food groups: proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals. A balanced diet fuels your workouts and promotes muscle recovery.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Hydration helps with digestion, nutrient absorption, and overall performance.

3. Prioritize Protein Intake

Protein is essential for muscle growth and recovery. Include lean meats, fish, eggs, and plant-based proteins like lentils and beans in your meals.

4. Control Portion Sizes

Watch your portion sizes to avoid overeating, especially with high-calorie foods. Focus on quality, not quantity.
